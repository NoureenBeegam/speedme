WORK WITH SMILE

Speed Me ships maintenance is a young company focused in engineering, procurement,
and contracting for industrial insulations and marine accommodation for ships and offshore vessels.
We integrate our operations to understand and provide the type service from the customer's perspective.
We are capable to handle marine repair and conversion, industrial insulations and cladding and modular fabrication.

Speed Me have established a cordial relationship with our local and international vendors
who are like minded and share the same views for quality and standards in engineering.

We are committed to quality, health, safety, and environmental requirements to be established in all our workplaces.

ABOUT US

We, Speed Me ships maintenance, marine division of Golden palace group of companies Established in 2006, as a service provider of transportation. Golden Palace is one of the most developing companies in the field of interior out fitting jobs, Advertising and transportation with its head office in Dubai.We are committed in offering quality service and maintaining exceptional standards in the field of accommodation out fitting jobs for our customers. With over more than 800staffs including drivers Golden Palace is blessed with the professionally skilled and experienced staffs that have shown their outstanding talents in the field of accommodation and interior jobs in the various projects and events in UAE. We have highly experienced man-power leading our service providing division for Insulation, Carpentry, Piping, Electrical, Flooring, Fabrication, and Insulation in repair division operating from our Dubai and Abu Dhabi offices. We also have quality assessed and experienced suppliers and sub contractors supporting us to enhance the work delivery.

OUR POLICY

Speed Me ships maintenance is an engineering company and will understand the type of service required by our customer's perspective. We will ensure the customer satisfaction is the top priority and our services shall render to the requirements without any compromise in quality and quantity. We will provide the best, most competitive and timely service to all our customers irrespective of any boundaries. Speed Me ships maintenance will achieve our aim with proper business plan, commitment, and communicate our views and values to all our employees and suppliers. We will establish a sound and trustworthy relationship with all our partners as they are an integral part of our quality commitment to our customer. Our employees are the face of the company and the true assets. We will expect high standards of personal conduct and trade knowledge. We will dedicate resources to continuously improve their skill by training and engage them as a team player. We will appreciate their efforts and recognize their success. We will work hard to continuously improve the standards of services in compliance with all the statutory and legal requirements. We will be sensible in contributing to the society and environment. We pledge to practice Quality, Health, Safety, and Environment requirements and comply the policies at all our work sites.
